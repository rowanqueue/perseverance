Mars is called the "Red Planet" because its regolith (surface material) is full of rust-colored iron oxide.
The Perseverance rover has 23 cameras and 2 microphones. It can take selfies if directed!
The atmosphere on Mars is 96% carbon dioxide and only .174% oxygen. Try breathing that!
On ancient Mars, water left its mark in the form of river deltas. The Jezero Crater is the site of one of those dried-out deltas.
Perseverance may look small in pictures, but it's the size of a car and weighs 2,260 pounds!
